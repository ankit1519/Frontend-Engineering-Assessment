Features of GloBud

Real-Time Monitoring

GloBud provides real-time insights into crucial factors that influence plant health, including soil moisture levels, sunlight exposure, and ambient temperature.

Intelligent Recommendations

Based on the collected data, GloBud offers intelligent recommendations tailored to each plant's specific needs. Receive timely suggestions on watering schedules, sunlight requirements, and more.

Effortless Control

Take control of your garden effortlessly with GloBud. Adjust settings, receive alerts, and manage your plants with just a few taps. The intuitive design makes navigation a breeze for users of all levels.

Visualize Growth Progress

Watch your garden flourish with GloBud's visual progress tracking. The device keeps a record of your plants' growth over time, allowing you to witness the positive impact of your care routine.
